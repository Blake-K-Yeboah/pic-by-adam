Frequently Asked Questions

Why Should I Get Professional Photos?

There are so many benefits to having professional photos of your organization and the products you offer on your website. ​

Here's 3:

1) A Picture Is Worth 1,000 Words.Photos enable you to show your clients and customers what you have to offer rather than simply telling them. Customers like to visualise what they're buying before making the dive into purchasing.

2) Your Media Represents Your Brand. Unprofessional photos on your website and social media presence will impact what potential customers and clients see first, before ever getting to know just how amazing you are.

3) Photos Are Versitile Assets. You can use use them to market on instagram, facebook, twitter and even through your emails. You can draw people into sections on your website that they otherwise wouldn't have taken a second look at. When done right, your photos will work for you.

What Is Your "24 Hour Delivery" Claim?

What's worse than having an amazing shoot, you get back to the office and recieve an email from your photographer with the subject line; "Finished Photos Ready in 4 Weeks"... ​
