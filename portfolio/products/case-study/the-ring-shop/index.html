The Ring Shop

In this case study, PicByAdam worked with TheRingShop to create a series of lifestyle sets that showcase the rings in an environment native to its colour palette and design as well as a series of shots with a hand model.

The rings i was sent to shoot

We constructed each hero shot’s set using common household items and dollar store props due to not having a budget for this project. We made 4 sets for the hero shots, one for each ring. Each was constructed from scratch based on elements found in the details of the ring. Due to the nature of the “Obsidian” ring. We decided to contrast the sleek all black design with a white cloud theme as seen later on in this case study.

In order to get unique lifestyle shots of these rings, we put them on our hand model and went to a local park. The reason for this decision was due to the fact that these rings are not only fashionable but are also made for everyday use to compliment any outfit.

Using creative lighting techniques to display the rings on structures such as trees and on stone as well as candid shots of hands being clasped and on skin, it highlights the details as well as the versatility of these rings.

This shoot not only showcases how stunning each item looks individually but also that they pack a punch when used together which is vital to selling more than one product at a time.
