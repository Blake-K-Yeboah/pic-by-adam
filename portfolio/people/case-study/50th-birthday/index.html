PEOPLE & EVENTS

Leanne's 50th Birthday

In this case study, PicByAdam worked with Leanne to capture her 50th Birthday Party celebrations. The goal of this project was to create memories that would allow Leanne to relive the party for years to come. There were several challenges we overcame throughout this project in order to generate over 230 stunning, edited memories (photos) for our client.

Leanne reached out to us regarding her upcoming birthday and upon hearing her vision we were thrilled to capture the event. We began by asking questions about the venue and occasion to give us a better understanding of how we can truly make these images special and to ensure we were prepared for the evening.

The next step was to head out to the venue a few nights early to see the conditions we’d be working under. We take our projects very seriously and want to guarantee that we have the right equipment for the photoshot.

We shot several practices shots adjusting our equipment to match the environment we were working in. Your event only happens once which is why we go to great lengths to ensure it’s captured exactly how you’d like it to be the FIRST TIME.

The night was a fantastic success and we managed to capture all the moments that matter most. It was an honour to be able to photograph smiles as well as show off the very talented band.
