Portraits & People

Shane King

In this case study, PicByAdam worked with Shane King to create a series of digital content establishing the “King” Brand across platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. This was done through a portrait photo series, automotive shots, and a sequence of compelling captions.

Shane King is an individual, located in Melbourne, Australia, that was looking to define his personal brand through his Instagram and social media profiles with images that embrace his personality while looking professional and interesting at the same time.

The goal of this project was to establish the “King” Brand across platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. This was done through a portrait photo series, automotive shots, and a sequence of compelling captions that resonate with not only who he is but also his intended audience.

He reached out to PicByAdam in late 2020, requesting to fly us down to complete this project over the span of 7 days at the start of January 2021. Complying with all local COVID-19 regulations, we accepted this project and began scouting locations in the city.

We approached this project open-minded and confident despite being in a new city environment. The first two days of shooting contained heavy rainfall which made it difficult to stick to the schedule however we used this to our advantage to add moody tones and ambiance to an otherwise average location. We value our ability to adapt and overcome situations that can easily be viewed as negative.

We don’t just view our clients as people we work with, we view them as friends and actively try to build relationships with each and every one of them. Many people are nervous to hire photographers because they envision it as several hours of awkward posing, rigid commands, and loud yelling when things don’t go right.

We walk into each shoot with only one goal in mind… “How can we make this experience as fun as possible for you our photographers”. Getting loose, playing music and good conversation stops our shoots from feeling draining and boring and instead transforms them into a positive experience that makes our clients look forward to working with us. This project was no different.
