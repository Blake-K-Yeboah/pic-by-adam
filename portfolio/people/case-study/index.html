Portraits & People

Shane King

In this case study, PicByAdam worked with Shane King to create a series of digital content establishing the “King” Brand across platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. This was done through a portrait photo series, automotive shots, and a sequence of compelling captions.

Shane King is an individual, located in Melbourne, Australia, that was looking to define his personal brand through his Instagram and social media profiles with images that embrace his personality while looking professional and interesting at the same time.
